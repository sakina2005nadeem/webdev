ARE YOU READY?

Cognizance is the conjugation of different technical skills important for everyone in the 21st century, providing a platform to students to promulgate their skills and aim to reach the pinnacle of their talents. Initiated in 2000, Cognizance has provided a podium for creative innovations and raw ideas to echo, receiving unique entries in each event of the competition. Tilln now, Cognizance has been a huge success with an overwhelming outreach from all over the city. We proudly present Cognizance 2021 with hopes to get an even more enthusiastic response.

EVENTS FOR EVERYONE

Programming

Programmers will be asked to solve complex algorithmic challenges in a limited amount of time, using the programming language of their choice in a team of 3.

Web Development

Students may create a website using any language of their choice on the given prompt.
Prompt: Create an interactive and user friendly landing page for a new clothing line.

Movie Making

Create a short animated advertisement for a Sportswear company in a team of 2.


IT Quiz

This event will be conducted in two phases: an Online Prelims (Crossword) followed by an Online Final Quiz via video conferencing. The date for the prelims will be informed to the teacher in charge through e-mail along with the link to participate.

Game Design

Students will need to submit a video of their game and a jury panel will nominate the best submissions for an interactive presentation at a conference, where a panel of judges choose the winners.

Photography

Participants would have to depict a story line with the help of 3 pictures clicked on the theme given on the spot in the allotted time. Pictures must be compiled into a collage with a JPG/JPEG format. Short description and topic of the story must also be given along with the collage.
